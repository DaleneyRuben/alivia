# Guest booking — no Patient accounts or authentication in v1

Booking requires only a Patient's name and phone number; there's no login, password, or account. Requiring an account would add friction at the exact moment a patient wants to book, for a low-frequency action (most patients book a handful of times a year) that doesn't yet need history or saved preferences. A Patient record is still stored server-side (contact info, appointment history) so the Doctor/Secretary have continuity — the missing piece is patient-facing auth, not patient data. WhatsApp effectively serves as the patient's channel for confirmations in place of an in-app account.
