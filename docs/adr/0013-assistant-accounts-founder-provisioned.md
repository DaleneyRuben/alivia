# Assistant accounts are founder-provisioned, not self-serve invited

Assistant accounts follow the same concierge pattern as Doctor accounts (see [0005](0005-concierge-doctor-onboarding.md)): the founder creates them from an Admin view, typically alongside the Doctor account when onboarding a practice, rather than a Doctor inviting their own Assistant from within the panel. This keeps account provisioning centralized in one place (the founder's roster of onboarded practices) instead of splitting it across a founder-run flow for Doctors and a self-serve flow for Assistants — consistent with how small the platform's account surface is expected to stay in v1. A self-serve "Doctor invites their Assistant" flow remains a candidate once the founder is provisioning enough practices that doing it by hand becomes the bottleneck.
